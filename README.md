# HR-Certification-Research-Polling

An annual poll tracking on which HR certification(s) professionals find most credible, HRCI (PHR/SPHR), SHRM (CP/SCP), conducted from 2017 to 2022. Note I am still cleaning up the data so nothing to see in this repo yet. 

**Project Description**

An annual poll tracking which HR certification(s) professionals find most credible — HRCI (PHR/SPHR), SHRM (CP/SCP), or equally credible, conducted among from 2017 to 2022.

**Data Summary**

• Years covered: 2017, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022

• Total respondents grew from ~30 (2019) to 44 (2022)

• Poll options: PHR/SPHR by HRCI, CP/SCP by SHRM, Equally Credible, No Preference, Elaborate/Complicated Opinions, Other

• Key finding: "I find them equally credible" has been the #1 response every year since 2019

**Workbook Structure**

• 2017 — Initial poll results (HRCI vs. No Preference vs. SHRM)

• 2019 — Expanded poll with 30 participants and 4 options

• 2020 — 42 respondents; added "elaborate opinions" option

• 2021 — 39 respondents; includes individual voter names and comments

• 2022 — 44 respondents; results marked confidential

• HRCI numbers — Snapshot of HRCI credential holders as of Feb 15, 2020 (137,267 total)

• Time Series — Multi-year consolidated view with vote counts, percentages, and rankings

• Chart — Visualization tab

• Notes — This sheet
